The following operations are supported:
1. Addition: '2 + 2'
2. Subtraction: '2 - 2'
3. Multiplication: '2 * 2'
4. Division: '2 / 2'
5. Exponential: '2 ^ 2'

The following functions are supported:
1. abs: absolute value
2. sin: sine
3. asin: inverse sine
4. sinh: hyperbolic sine
5. cos: cosine
6. acos: inverse cosine
7. cosh: hyperbolic cosine
8. tan: tangent
9. atan: inverse tangent
10. tanh: hyperbolic tangent
11. sqrt: square root
12. cbrt: cubic root
13. log: logarithmus naturalis (base e)
14. exp: euler's number raised to the power (e^x)
15. floor: nearest lower integer
